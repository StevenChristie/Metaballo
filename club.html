The Space / ρυθμός

An unmarked
room that
moves.

Behind the kitchen, through the door no one notices, lies a room built for a different kind of night. Floor-to-ceiling vinyl. Bar stools worn to the right shape. A sound system that was never meant to be quiet.

It is the same philosophy as the restaurant — transformation through ritual — but the ritual here is movement, music, and the long arc of a late evening that refuses to end at a reasonable hour.

Resident DJs. Occasional guests. Always vinyl.
