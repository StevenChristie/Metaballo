The Philosophy
μεταβάλλω

To change, to turn about. Metaballo represents the profound act of “throwing over”—transforming raw elements into refined experiences through a radical shift in perspective. Here, the flame does not just cook; it transforms. The spirit does not just mix; it evolves.

Heritage / γένεσις

Where flame
becomes craft

Metaballo was founded on a singular conviction — that food is not made, but transformed. Born of Cretan kitchens and refined through decades on the grill, our cuisine borrows from the Mediterranean's oldest traditions and reframes them through the lens of contemporary restraint.

Fire, salt, smoke, sea. Four elements that have fed Greece for three thousand years. We treat them not as ingredients but as inheritances — passed hand to hand, kitchen to kitchen, generation to generation.

Every plate that leaves our kitchen has passed through three hands and seven flames. Every spirit at the bar has been chosen for its capacity to evolve.
